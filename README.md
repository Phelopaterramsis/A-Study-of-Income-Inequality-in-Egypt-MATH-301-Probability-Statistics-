## ðŸ“Ž Conclusion  
Our findings confirm the presence of **systemic income inequality** in Egypt influenced by gender biases, educational access, and household structure.  
Women earn substantially less than men, education access strongly impacts income distribution, and wealthy households surprisingly tend to be larger.

This project highlights the need for evidence-based policies aimed at improving educational access, reducing gender discrimination, and addressing structural inequalities across socioeconomic classes.
